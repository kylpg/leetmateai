- ‚úÖ Only accessed when you actively use LeetMate features
- ‚úÖ Sent directly to your chosen AI provider for processing
- ‚úÖ Never stored permanently by LeetMate
- ‚úÖ Never shared with third parties beyond your chosen AI provider

### Local Storage
We store locally on your device:
- **API keys** for your chosen AI providers (encrypted by Chrome)
- **Extension preferences** (selected AI provider, UI settings)
- **Usage statistics** (number of hints requested, for your reference only)

All local data:
- ‚úÖ Stays on your device
- ‚úÖ Is encrypted by Chrome's secure storage
- ‚úÖ Can be deleted by uninstalling the extension
- ‚úÖ Is never transmitted to LeetMate servers
